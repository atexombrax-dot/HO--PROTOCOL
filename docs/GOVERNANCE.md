HOÏ Governance

Overview

HOÏ Governance is the decentralized decision-making framework of the HOÏ-PROTOCOL ecosystem. It allows community members to participate in the evolution, management, and strategic direction of the protocol.

---

Principles

The governance system is based on:

- Transparency
- Accountability
- Inclusion
- Community participation
- Sustainable development
- Pan-African cooperation

---

Governance Objectives

The governance framework aims to:

- Guide ecosystem development
- Allocate community resources
- Approve strategic initiatives
- Protect the long-term vision of HOÏ
- Encourage active participation

---

Governance Participants

HOÏ Holders

Token holders can:

- Vote on proposals
- Participate in consultations
- Influence ecosystem decisions

Contributors

Contributors may:

- Submit improvement proposals
- Participate in working groups
- Assist in ecosystem development

Governance Council

A governance council may be established to:

- Coordinate community initiatives
- Monitor protocol compliance
- Facilitate proposal review

The council remains accountable to the community.

---

Proposal Process

Step 1 – Proposal Submission

A community member submits a proposal.

Step 2 – Community Discussion

The proposal is reviewed and discussed publicly.

Step 3 – Voting Period

Eligible participants vote.

Step 4 – Implementation

Approved proposals are executed according to governance rules.

---

Voting System

Voting may be used for:

- Protocol upgrades
- Treasury allocations
- Strategic partnerships
- Community programs
- Ecosystem expansion initiatives

---

Treasury Governance

Community-approved funds may support:

- Research and development
- Education initiatives
- Ecosystem infrastructure
- Strategic partnerships
- Social impact projects

---

Transparency

Governance records should be publicly accessible, including:

- Proposals
- Voting outcomes
- Treasury reports
- Governance decisions

---

Future Development

Future governance enhancements may include:

- On-chain voting
- Delegated voting
- Regional governance structures
- AI-assisted governance analytics
